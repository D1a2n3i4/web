Our Services

Medical Services

Saylani Welfare International Trust runs medical facilities and clinics to offer healthcare services to those who cannot afford them. They provide free medical check-ups, consultations, medicines, and surgeries to disadvantaged communities. Additionally, they organize medical camps and outreach programs in remote areas to address healthcare needs effectively.

Food Distribution

Saylani runs extensive food distribution programs to combat hunger and food insecurity. They provide cooked meals, food rations, and food parcels to disadvantaged individuals and families across Pakistan, particularly during times of crisis or emergencies.

Education

Saylani Welfare International Trust places a strong emphasis on education, believing it to be a fundamental tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. They have established numerous schools and educational institutions in underprivileged areas, providing free or subsidized education to children who would otherwise lack access to quality schooling.

Clean Water Initiatives

Saylani Welfare International Trust recognizes the critical importance of access to clean water for health and sanitation. To address this need, they have implemented various projects to provide clean drinking water to communities facing water scarcity or contamination issues.

General Welfare Programs

In addition to their core focus areas, Saylani Welfare International Trust engages in a range of general welfare programs to address the diverse needs of communities. These initiatives may include shelter provision for the homeless, marriage support for financially constrained families, disaster relief efforts during natural calamities, and rehabilitation programs for individuals facing social challenges.

Introduction about Saylani

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a beacon of hope and compassion, illuminating the lives of the underprivileged with its unwavering commitment to humanitarian aid. Established in 1999 by Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqui, Saylani Welfare International Trust has emerged as a prominent non-profit organization dedicated to alleviating poverty, providing healthcare, promoting education, and offering disaster relief to those in need across Pakistan and beyond. With a philosophy rooted in the principles of selfless service and social justice, Saylani Welfare International Trust has touched the lives of millions, embodying the spirit of compassion and empathy in its tireless pursuit of a better, more equitable world.
